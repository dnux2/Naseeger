NASEEGER — نَسِيجَر
Luxury Saudi Thobe House · 

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Overview :
NASEEGER is a high-end fashion web experience built for a fictional luxury thobe brand inspired by the architectural patterns of At-Turaif, Diriyah. The project explores how Saudi cultural identity can be expressed through modern digital design — dark, cinematic, and deeply rooted in heritage.

----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------
 Features

- Full-screen fabric showcase with stacked card drag interaction
- Real fabric & thobe product imagery
- Diriyah castle atmospheric background
- Glass-morphism navigation with brand logo
- Heritage section with craftsman photography
- Collections, testimonials, and about sections
- Smooth scroll animations powered by Framer Motion
- Fully responsive (mobile → desktop)
- Gold & lavender color palette with Najdi geometric patterns

 Brand Identity :

**NASEEGER** — نَسِيجَر — derives from the Arabic root *نسج* meaning "to weave." The brand weaves together three threads: the ancient mud-brick architecture of Diriyah, the golden heritage of Saudi craftsmanship, and the refined sensibility of the modern Gulf gentleman.
